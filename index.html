getsb2makes sb2 files from project IDs.

Just append a project ID or URL after the /.

http://getsb2.herokuapp.com/15832807
http://getsb2.herokuapp.com/http://scratch.mit.edu/projects/15832807/

You can also add ?zip to download the contents of the sb2 as a zip file.

http://getsb2.herokuapp.com/15832807?zip
